Why Support Rural Women & Youth?

High unemployment, limited access to finance, and low agricultural productivity continue to limit economic opportunity for rural communities. AYADI implements a phased economic empowerment model that builds long-term resilience and income growth.

Our Empowerment Model

Train

Skills development for women and youth in agriculture, entrepreneurship and financial literacy.

Finance

Access to savings groups, microfinance support and cooperative strengthening.

Produce & Reinvest

Market linkage support and reinvestment strategies to ensure sustainable income growth.
